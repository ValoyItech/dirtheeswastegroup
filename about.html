Our Story

From a vision of sustainable waste management to a leading e-waste solutions provider.

2025 - Foundation

Dirthees Waste Group was founded with a clear mission: to address the growing e-waste crisis in South Africa while creating economic opportunities for local communities. We started with a small team passionate about environmental sustainability and social impact.

Early Growth

We established our first collection and processing facility in Alexandra, focusing on building relationships with local businesses and institutions. Our commitment to transparency and responsible practices quickly earned us recognition in the community.

Expanding Services

We expanded our services to include comprehensive e-waste management solutions: from collection and sorting to advanced recycling and material recovery. Our team grew to include specialists in various aspects of electronic waste processing.

Today & Beyond

Today, we're proud to be a trusted partner for businesses seeking responsible e-waste management solutions. We continue to innovate and expand our services while maintaining our core commitment to environmental protection and community development.
